Allu Arjun

About

Allu Arjun (born 8 April 1982) is an Indian actor who works in Telugu cinema. He is one of the highest-paid actors in Indian cinema and has been featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list since 2014.[1][2] Referred to as the "Icon Star," he is known for his versatility and acclaimed dancing abilities.[3][4][5] He has appeared in over 21 films and is a recipient of several accolades including a National Film Award, six Filmfare Awards, and three Nandi Awards
